Thiruvananthapuram


Napier Museum

Named after Lord Napier, this art and history museum was established in 1855. An exemplary example of gothic architecture-style, Napier Museum boasts greatly of its natural air conditioning system.

Kovalam Beach

Epicentre of tourist flow, Kovalam Beach needs no introduction. This water delight seizes the top position in the list of tourist places in Trivandrum. Often crowded with both local and foreign tourists.

Ponmudi

Its narrow, meandering pathways, dark woods and soothing climate make it a splendid location to spend a holiday at. A multitude of colourful butterflies, springs and rapids, myriad species wild flowers and the deer park nearby are the key attraction of this fascinating hill station.
